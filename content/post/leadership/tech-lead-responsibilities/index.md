The tech lead role is a unique position that bridges technical excellence and people leadership. Let's explore what it takes to succeed.

## What Does a Tech Lead Do?

A tech lead wears many hats:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│              Tech Lead                   │
├──────────┬──────────┬──────────┬────────┤
│Technical │  People  │ Process  │Project │
│ Leader   │  Leader  │ Leader   │Leader  │
└──────────┴──────────┴──────────┴────────┘
```

## Technical Responsibilities

### Architecture Decisions
- Make and communicate technical decisions
- Balance short-term needs with long-term vision
- Review and guide technical designs

### Code Quality
- Set coding standards
- Conduct code reviews
- Mentor on best practices

### Technical Debt Management
- Identify and prioritize technical debt
- Balance new features with maintenance
- Advocate for necessary refactoring

## People Responsibilities

### Mentorship
Help team members grow:
- Regular 1:1s focused on growth
- Pair programming sessions
- Knowledge sharing initiatives

### Team Health
- Create psychological safety
- Address conflicts early
- Celebrate wins and learn from failures

### Communication Bridge
- Translate business needs to technical requirements
- Explain technical constraints to stakeholders
- Shield team from unnecessary interruptions

## Common Challenges

### The Coding Trap
Many new tech leads struggle to let go of coding:

| Time Allocation | Junior Tech Lead | Experienced Tech Lead |
|----------------|------------------|----------------------|
| Coding | 70% | 30% |
| Reviews/Mentoring | 15% | 35% |
| Meetings/Planning | 15% | 35% |

### Decision Fatigue
You'll be asked to make many decisions. Learn to:
- Delegate decisions when appropriate
- Document decision rationale
- Accept that not every decision will be perfect

## Key Skills to Develop

1. **Systems thinking** - See the big picture
2. **Communication** - Explain complex topics simply
3. **Influence without authority** - Lead by example
4. **Time management** - Prioritize ruthlessly
5. **Emotional intelligence** - Read and respond to team dynamics

## Conclusion

Being a tech lead is about multiplying your impact through others. Focus on enabling your team to do their best work, and you'll succeed.
